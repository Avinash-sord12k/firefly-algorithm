Firefly Algorithm Visualisation

Hey there! Have you heard about the firefly algorithm? It's a cool optimization technique that's inspired by the behavior of fireflies. These little critters are attracted to each other based on brightness, but here's the twist - their brightness changes with distance! So they're actually drawn to the fireflies that are the brightest in their relative vicinity. Pretty neat, huh? If you're interested in learning more, check out the wikipedia page. And by the way, you can play around with the values to see how it all works. Have fun!
